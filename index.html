Tips & Tricks

Master Forex trading with proven strategies and expert insights from Wasim Forex.

📊
Risk Management First
Never risk more than 1–2% of your account per trade. Protect capital to stay in the game long-term. Professional traders survive drawdowns — amateurs don't.
🕐
Trade the Best Sessions
London and New York session overlaps (8 AM–12 PM EST) offer the highest liquidity and tightest spreads. This is where the real money is made.
📈
Follow the Trend
The trend is your friend. Use higher timeframes (H4, Daily) to identify direction, then enter on lower timeframes (H1, M15) for precision entries.
🎯
1:3 Risk-to-Reward
Always aim for minimum 1:3 RRR. Even with 40% win rate, you'll be profitable. Let winners run and cut losers early — this is the golden rule.
🧠
Trading Psychology
Fear and greed are your biggest enemies. Stick to your trading plan, avoid revenge trading, and never chase a missed entry. Discipline beats talent.
🔍
Support & Resistance
Strong S/R zones are where banks place their orders. Master these levels and you'll understand where price is heading before it moves.
