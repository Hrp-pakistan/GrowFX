What are Forex signals and how do they work?

Forex signals are trade recommendations that tell you exactly when to buy or sell a currency pair. Each signal includes an Entry Price (where to open the trade), a Stop Loss (where to exit if the trade goes wrong), and a Take Profit (your target profit level). At GrowFX, our signals are generated through technical and fundamental analysis by experienced traders — giving you a clear, actionable trade idea without needing to do the analysis yourself.

How do I receive GrowFX signals?

All signals are delivered through our official Telegram channel. Simply click the "Join Telegram" button and you'll be instantly added to the group. You'll receive a notification on your phone the moment a new signal is published — including all Entry, Stop Loss, and Take Profit levels. We also post trade updates (e.g., move SL to breakeven) and results in the same channel.

What currency pairs do you cover?

We cover all major, minor, and select exotic pairs. Our most active pairs include:

• Majors: EUR/USD, GBP/USD, USD/JPY, USD/CHF, AUD/USD, USD/CAD, NZD/USD
• Crosses: GBP/JPY, EUR/JPY, GBP/AUD, EUR/GBP
• Commodities: XAU/USD (Gold), XAG/USD (Silver), WTI Oil

Signals are based on highest-probability setups — we only send signals when the analysis is clear and the risk/reward is favorable.

Yes — completely free. Our Telegram channel is 100% free to join. You'll get access to all signals, market analysis, and trade updates at no cost. We believe in building trust through results first. Simply tap the Join Telegram button and you're in — no registration, no credit card, no hidden fees.

What is the win rate of your signals?

Our verified win rate over the last 90 days stands at 94%. We achieve this by only signaling high-probability setups with clear structure and a minimum 1:3 risk-to-reward ratio. Not every signal wins — no one can guarantee 100% — but our strict analysis process ensures we maintain consistent profitability. Historical performance reports are available in the Telegram channel.

How many signals do you send per day?

We send an average of 3–7 signals per day, depending on market conditions. Quality is always prioritized over quantity — we never force trades when the market is unclear or conditions are unfavorable. During high-impact news events (e.g., NFP, FOMC), we may pause signals to protect our members from unpredictable volatility.

Do I need trading experience to follow your signals?

No prior experience is needed. Our signals are designed to be simple to follow — even for complete beginners. Each signal tells you exactly: what pair to trade, whether to buy or sell, where to enter, where to place your stop loss, and where to take profit. We also post educational content in the channel to help new traders understand the "why" behind each trade. Beginners are welcome.

What broker should I use to trade your signals?

Our signals are broker-independent — you can use any regulated Forex broker that offers the pairs we cover. We recommend brokers with tight spreads, fast execution, and proper regulation (FCA, CySEC, ASIC). Popular choices among our members include IC Markets, Exness, FP Markets, and XM. Always ensure your broker is regulated before depositing funds. Never trade with money you cannot afford to lose.
